My love for art has been instilled within me since I was a young boy.
I would say I have to thank my parents for my unique fashion choices and using me as their personal model.
However, they've created a monster because I literally cant stop thinking of what type of clothing I'll be putting on next.

Daniel Arsham and Sean Wotherspoon are two creative designers that inspire me every day.
They both have worked for multi billionaire companies like Nike and Adidas along with multi platinum musicians.
Their thought process and creativite color palette drew me in from the very beginning.
